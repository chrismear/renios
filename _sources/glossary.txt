========
Glossary
========

.. glossary::
   
   Ren'Py
      An open source visual novel engine that is powerful and mature. Find out more at `the Ren'Py home page <http://renpy.org>`_.
   
   Xcode
      Apple's tool for developing and building iOS apps. You can download it for free: `Xcode on the Mac App Store <https://itunes.apple.com/gb/app/xcode/id497799835?mt=12>`_
