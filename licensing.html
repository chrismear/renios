Licensing¶

The full details of Ren’iOS’s license are in the file LICENSE.md.

Some portions of Ren’iOS are based on works licensed under the LGPL 2.1. Therefore these portions must also licensed under the LGPL 2.1.

Because of those portions, the single license that applies to Ren’iOS taken as a whole is the LGPL Version 2.1.

In addition to that license, those portions of Ren’iOS that are not required to be licensed under the LGPL are also individually licensed under the MIT (Expat) license.

Each file/directory in Ren’iOS contains a permissions notice that indicates which license(s) apply to that file/directory.

An application binary that you build using Ren’iOS should not itself need to be licensed under the LGPL.
